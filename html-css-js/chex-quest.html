Origin

This was definitely an original idea (what even is a doom?!) created by Digital Cage as a Chex cereal promotion. It was(n't) a total conversion of the more violent video Doom. This was targeted at children from Ages 6-9. But has a surprisingly large fanbase even to this very day.

About

Set on a distant planet named Bazoik, the game follows the Chex Warrior, a soldier clad in a Chex-shaped suit of armor, as he foils the invasion of the planet by the 'Flemoids' a species of slimy, green invertebrates, who have infested the planet and captured many helpless colonists, whom the Chex Warrior must save. His main weapons are devices called "zorchers", which teleports his enemies to their home dimension. Their principal weapon is the use of mucus as a projectile.
